
Use the format below for PCs the you have admin rights to
pcname,,

Use the format below for PCs that you must supply credentials to audit (PCs not on your domain)
pcname,domain\username,password

Use PC names - NOT IPAddresses (you may need to add the names/IPs to your hosts file for this)
Do not leave a blank line at the end of the file.
You CAN mix and match domain and not domain PCs in the one file.
